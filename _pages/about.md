I'm Siddhant, a Computer Engineering junior at UMass Amherst (graduating Spring 2027) with a minor in Engineering Management. I work across the stack, from PCB design and embedded firmware to full-stack web development and machine learning, and I'm most energized by problems that sit at the intersection of hardware and software.

Outside of coursework, I'm a researcher at the UMass Human Performance Lab studying how truck drivers interact with automated vehicle technology through VR simulation, and an AI Software Engineer Fellow at Minds With Purpose Foundation, where I build mobile and web features for a social accountability platform.

I care about building things that work reliably and serve people well. Whether that's a timing-attack-hardened server, a CNN deployed on embedded hardware, or a React Native profile system with Apple Wallet integration, I like work that has real stakes.

When I'm not at a keyboard, I'm probably playing guitar, watching cricket, or building something out of LEGO.
